Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 29, 2025

QR Scan ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

1. No Personal Data Collected

QR Scan does not collect, store, or share any personal data. You are not required to create an account or provide any information to use the app.

2. Camera Access

The app uses your device's camera solely for scanning QR codes. We do not record or store any camera footage or images.

3. QR Code Generation

Any data you enter to generate a QR code is processed locally on your device. It is not sent to any server or stored.

4. No Internet Tracking

The app does not track your activity, location, or behavior. There are no analytics or tracking services embedded.

5. Children's Privacy

Our app is safe for users of all ages and does not collect information from children under the age of 13.

6. Changes to This Policy

If we update this Privacy Policy, we will notify users by updating the effective date above. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
